About

Konsthack.github.io is a website for the Hackerspace of Konstfack (University of arts crafts and design) in Stockholm.

  1. Code of Conduct
    • The hackspace is an inclusive space where everyone is welcome regardless of age, ethnicity, gender, sexuality or religion. We aim to discuss and learn more about technology in an environment of mutual respect, tolerance and encouragement. We want all participants to have an enjoyable and fulfilling experience. Therefore, all participants are expected to be polite and respectful towards each other.
  2. Behavior-driven development
    • We explore and try to develop a test- and behavior-driven development method (TDD or BTD). It is to try make design, tools and learning processes more transparency and shareable. All steps in the process are contectulized as a test and the projects are iterated in small understandable steps.
  3. Hacker Ethics
    • Our hacker ethics involve Sharing, Openness, Decentralization, Free access to computers, Feminism and World Improvement
    • https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hacker_ethic#The_hacker_ethics
  4. When
    • We meet every Monday at 15 PM onwards for a couple of hours
  5. Where
    • The hackerspace is located at the second floor in the middle of the media block in the the art department video and data lab.
