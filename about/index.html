About

Konsthack.github.io is a documentation for the Hackerspace of Konstfack (University of arts crafts and design) in Stockholm.

  • Hacker Ethics and Feminism
  • Code of Conduct
  • Behavior-driven development
    • We explore a test- and behavior-driven development method (TTD pr BTD) to make design, learning processes more transparency so tools and process are shared.
